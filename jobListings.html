Job Overview


As GoFor continues its rapid growth we are looking for a Product Owner. As a Product Owner you will focus on liaison between Product Management and Research and Development Departments whilst representing customer needs and ensuring progress and accuracy of in-work product features.


You will also help marshal the stories through the R&D development process to ensure the highest priority items are being done at the right time and they are being released to market in a timely fashion.


We are looking for a tenured individual who can view an entire feature and break it down into smaller pieces. The successful candidate must thrive on an ever changing environment, have the ability to shift focus while maintaining progress within ongoing work items.


In this role as a Product Owner, you will primarily:


  • Work alongside Product Management to understand and define upcoming product updates / features.
  • Represent the target persona through from conception to delivery
  • Inspire engineers to think about the customer experience as they craft the GoFor workflows.
  • Distill complex features so they are digestible to multiple levels of the business. Display attention to detail and a willingness to explain to others, both verbally and in writing to gain buy-in.
  • Own the product backlog. Work with R&D to maintain a healthy backlog of prioritized work items, ensuring the most valuable are being defined and addressed.
  • Identifying tangible elements of value to end users and deliver to market in a Continuous Delivery methodology.
  • Get into the field, sit with our customers to understand their core business needs
  • Participate in Executive level client conversations to capture and distill their business requirements.

  • Understanding of the principles of Product Development, Agile and SCRUM framework
